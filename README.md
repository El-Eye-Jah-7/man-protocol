# The MAN Protocol (`$MAN`)

> **There is only one humanity. There is only one coin.**

The MAN Protocol is an open-source, sovereign, decentralized economic network built to establish a perfectly equal, uncheatable, and non-inflationary Universal Basic Income (UBI) for all human beings. 

---

## 🌌 Core Philosophy

Modern financial systems favor centralized manipulation, speculative hoarding, and infinite supply inflation. The MAN Protocol resets this paradigm by anchoring economic value to the only resource distributed equally to every human being on Earth: **Time**. 

By merging absolute asset scarcity with device-independent biometric tracking and a closed-loop marketplace, MAN ensures that a billionaire and an everyday citizen stand on perfectly equal financial footing. Value inside this ecosystem is driven entirely by real human labor, goods, and services—completely independent of traditional fiat money or banking institutions. Wealth cannot be inherited or locked in dead wallets; it perpetually recycles to sustain the living.

---

## 🛠️ System Architecture & Tokenomics

### 1. Absolute Token Scarcity (`Supply = 1.00`)
* **The Hard Cap:** Exactly **1.00 MAN** coin exists in total. 
* **Zero Inflation:** No additional supply can ever be minted, created, or generated by any entity. 
* **Hyper-Divisibility:** To scale across a global population without printing tokens, the network relies entirely on an infinitely expanding fractional math framework.

### 2. The Infinite Decimal Engine (Dynamic Slicing)
Standard cryptocurrency tokens are locked at 18 decimal places. MAN implements a custom computing logic that continuously sharpens the mathematical knife to slice the existing 1.00 coin into infinitely smaller pieces.
* **The Slicing Rule:** The smart contract adds exactly **one extra decimal place every passing second**. 
* **Exponential Divisibility:** Starting from base 18 decimals at launch, the currency automatically expands to the **86,418th decimal place** by the end of Day 1.
* **The Limitless Pool:** This mathematical engine ensures that the system will never run out of units, even as the global population scales. There is always a smaller, viable fraction available to distribute.

### 3. The 24-Hour Fairness Distribution Pool
To completely eliminate time-zone bias, network crowding, or transaction "gas wars":
* **The Accumulation:** The daily allocation of MAN fractions is calculated over a full 86,400-second cycle and held securely inside the protocol vault.
* **The Equal Split:** At midnight UTC, the contract takes the day's total pool and checks the exact number of unique, verified humans ($N$) who checked in during that 24-hour window.
* **Simultaneous Payout:** The daily pool is split perfectly and equally ($Pool / N$) among all participants. The rate received is completely independent of *when* a user completes their verification during the day.

## 🛡️ Identity & Anti-Sybil Security Matrix

To achieve pure human equality, the system must detect and block automated AI bots, deepfakes, multi-nationality passport exploitation, and device farming.

### 1. Multi-Layered "Proof-of-Life" Verification
Every 24 hours, users must open the app and pass a randomized, interactive liveness checkpoint to receive their daily share:
* **Liveness Prompts:** Unpredictable animation cues (e.g., blinking, head rotations, reading live phrases) to block static photos or video loops.
* **Biometric Mapping:** End-to-end encrypted live face and voice matrix matching.

### 2. Device-Independent 10-Finger Camera Scan
To bypass the privacy lockdowns of native smartphone manufacturers (which hide raw biometric data from developers) and to solve the identical twin loophole:
* **The Twin Proof:** Identical twins share DNA and facial structures, but they *always* possess unique fingerprints. The fingerprint serves as the absolute root anchor of human identity.
* **Custom Computer Vision SDK:** Users place their hands over an on-screen visual guide. The app forces the display to max brightness and engages the rear camera to take a macro-photographic snapshot of a **full 10-finger hand scan**. 
* **The Hand Matrix:** By capturing all 10 fingers simultaneously, the software calculates a single, unified biometric hash. This eliminates the "finger rotation" cheat where one user attempts to open 10 accounts using 10 separate fingers.

### 3. Automated Asset Repossession Protocol
Because the custom camera scan owns the raw biometric data, it hashes and cross-checks every hand matrix against a global ledger.
* **The Match Flag:** If an incoming 10-finger matrix matches a profile already registered in the ledger (even if the user attempts to sign up with a different name, device, or secondary passport), a critical fraud event is triggered.
* **The Clawback:** The duplicate account is permanently destroyed. The smart contract executes a protocol-level clawback, automatically **repossessing 100% of the fractional MAN** from the fraudulent profile and returning it straight to the public distribution pool.

## 🛒 The Closed-Loop Hyper-Deflationary Marketplace

The MAN Protocol does not operate as a financial stock to be speculated on; it is built exclusively as an active, self-sustaining medium of exchange powered directly by the utility of human labor and enterprise.

### 1. Total Banking Isolation
* **Closed-Loop System:** MAN is strictly non-exchangeable with traditional fiat banking systems, credit institutions, or outside cryptocurrency exchanges. 
* **Internal Trade Only:** All peer-to-peer commerce, utility payments, and merchant transactions occur exclusively inside the official MAN App or Web Portal. This completely immunizes the network from outside hedge funds, market dumps, and speculative manipulation.

### 2. The Hyper-Deflationary Price-Drop Engine
To guarantee financial freedom as token slices shrink due to population growth, the internal marketplace prices are algorithmically governed by a compounding deflationary mechanism:
* **The Velocity Gap:** While token slices expand by 86,400 zeros per day, ecosystem prices are hardcoded to drop by **100,000 zeros per day**.
* **Increasing Purchasing Power:** Because price tags shrink faster than the token distribution slices do, the relative purchasing power of a single day of human time increases daily. This intentional mathematical gap guarantees a massive compound increase in global purchasing power, ensures basic needs require increasingly microscopic fractions over time, and exponentially maximizes the volume of unspent coins available for repossession upon a user's death.

### 3. The Death Audit (Perpetual Circulation)
To prevent the total supply of 1.00 MAN from being locked away in abandoned wallets across generations:
* **The Audit Trigger:** If a user fails to complete their daily Proof-of-Life check for a legally defined grace period, they are flagged as inactive or deceased.
* **Unused Coin Reclamation:** The smart contract executes a final sweep of the wallet. **Only the unused, unspent coins** remaining in their personal balance are repossessed and returned to the public distribution pool.
* **Protection of Commerce:** Any coins that the user already exchanged for real goods or services remain completely untouched within the merchant network. Wealth cannot be hoarded or hooped through inheritance; it is constantly recycling to sustain the living.
